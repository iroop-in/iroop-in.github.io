"Let the people be rewarded for good and punished for bad"

Project Description

  • Challenge
  • Deliverables

Firstly, after checking the brief, I tried to figure out myself what is social rating system? for whom are we designing it? what they need? any existing srs? any opportunity gaps? I just spent some time doing small time research understanding what this thing is and what all things will be involved in this then how should we start to achieve this.

Process

Breaking things down

So what things I need to do know? I did some small research , discovered things about this, got to know some news things and how we can solve this. Now, let's take steps how to solve this, firstly let's break this down into small pieces, so that we find the objectives and goals for this and how can we achieve this. What will be the requirements for this and all.

Goal

To encourage responsible living among the citizens

Break down the goal

Possible way to encourage responsible living → Reward the good and punish the bad → By Checking their respective ratings → By Monitoring their daily life routines of citizens → By completing the tasks or following what government is saying → By finding the issues in the society and and finding a way to solve them by creating tasks → By monitoring the parameters of the society

Objectives

Monitor the parameters
Find the issues
Define tasks
Rate the citizens based on score and task achievement
Reward and Punish citizens

Challenges

Find parameters for monitoring & scoring the citizens
Find issues and prioritise them
Find the solutions for the issues
Create tasks
Calculate the scores
Based on scores, Define rewards and punishments

Finding the Parameters

Going through many references, online research, I narrowed many parameters into 8 main parameters. There are two types of intersecting parameters we are dealing with - For Monitoring , For Individual. There are also subparameters under each parameter.
The reality of gathering parameters in real life is also explained later.

N.B. here

1. The parameters selected here can be monitored in reality.
2. Citizens should be able to alter the rates of the parameters.

Parameters for Monitoring & Individual

Civic Sense
Regulatory and Legal Violations
Legal history
HealthCare
Skill development / Employment
Socio-Economic Responsiblities
Environmental Conservation Efforts
Political Data

Additional Parameters for Individual

Identity and Employment information
Financial Information
